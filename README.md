## Hey, I'm Scarecrow! ✨
_Look for a woman in a long, red dress, wandering through the grey blocks in that peculiar time where the night meets the morning. Many will refer to her as the "witch" or "the mom of the group", but you shall proceed carefully if you ever shall see her_

In an alternate reality, my name is Matylda and I'm a university student majoring in Computer Science.  I'm an alumni of the 4th edition of "Dziewczyny w Grze!" at CD Projekt Red and game development is my greatest passion. I spend most of my days discovering peculiar places in my city, writing and desiging quests. And ofcourse - playing games!


## Doing side quests as a way of living 🎮
Here is a short list of the technology I mainly use these days!
- Unreal Engine (Blueprints & C++) - my main engine at this moment
- Unity
- Aurora Engine (NWN2 Toolset)
- .NET and C#
- Python
