## Hey, I'm Scarecrow! ✨
_Look for a woman in a long, red dress, wandering through the grey blocks in that peculiar time where the night meets the morning. Many will refer to her as the "witch" or "the mom of the group", but you shall proceed carefully if you ever see her_

In an alternate reality, my name is Matylda and I'm a university student majoring in Computer Science. I'm the alumni of the 4th edition of "Dziewczyny w Grze!" at CD Projekt Red and game development is my greatest passion. I spend most of my days discovering peculiar places in my city, writing and designing quests. And of course - playing games!

## Doing side quests as a way of living 🎮
Here is a short list of the technology I mainly use these days!
- **Unreal Engine** (Blueprints & C++) - my main engine, I'm currently developing 2 games with it
- **Unity** - mostly for prototypes, I'm working on a platformer game as well
- **Aurora Engine** (NWN2 Toolset) - occasionally for implementing quests
- **.NET and C#** - small experiments with math visualisations
- **Python** - mostly for algorithmics at uni
